About

I'm a Software QA Engineer with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, currently working for a Fortune 500 enterprise. My work is focused on ensuring quality, adding accessibility to digital products, and implementing AI solutions. I've authored a book on accessibility techniques in software development, published and reviewed scholarly articles, and had the privilege of judging hackathons. I'm also an active member of several professional organizations and hold multiple industry certifications, always looking to stay ahead in the evolving world of tech.
